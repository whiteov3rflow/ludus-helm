Having issues?
  1. Install the WireGuard client for your operating system from wireguard.com/install
  2. Open WireGuard and click "Import Tunnel(s) from File"
  3. Select the downloaded .conf file
  4. Click "Activate" to connect
  5. If connection fails, check that no other VPN is active and that UDP port 51820 is not blocked by your firewall
